Our Work

Our Industrial Expertise in Electrical Infrastructure

At S.K. Urja, our commitment to excellence and innovation in the electrical and solar energy sectors is evident in the diversity of our projects. Our expertise extends across a broad spectrum of installations and solutions, including Highway Street Lighting, where we provide reliable illumination for major roadways, enhancing safety and visibility for drivers. Our high mast lighting solutions are engineered for maximum efficiency, suitable for large, open spaces such as stadiums, ports, and industrial complexes, ensuring top-notch lighting coverage.


In addition to lighting solutions, we specialize in substation installations. Our substations form a crucial part of the electrical grid, transforming and distributing electricity with precision and security. The transformers we deploy are designed for durability and optimal performance, capable of handling extensive power loads required by industries and residential zones alike. Beyond traditional power, our Solar Rooftop installations represent our commitment to sustainable energy, harnessing solar power to reduce carbon emissions and offer energy independence to businesses and households.


Our partnerships with government contractors like म्हाडा and NIMADA underscore our credibility and dedication to quality, making us a trusted name in electrical contracting across diverse domains. At S.K. Urja, we are continuously evolving to meet the demands of tomorrow’s energy landscape, striving for efficiency, sustainability, and reliability in all our projects.
